RecipeMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

RecipeMind ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Recipes & Data You Create: All recipes, meal plans, pantry items, and grocery lists are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage. We do not transmit this data to our servers.
  • AI API Key: If you choose to use AI features, you provide your own Anthropic API key. This key is stored securely on your device only and is never sent to us.
  • Voice Data: If you use voice cooking mode, audio is processed entirely on-device using Apple's Speech framework. No audio is transmitted externally.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell personal information. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not have access to your recipes, meal plans, or any content you create in the app.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to use AI features, your prompts are sent directly from your device to Anthropic's API using your own API key. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API requests.

Children's Privacy

RecipeMind is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The updated date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance.
